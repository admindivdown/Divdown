Frequently Asked Questions
Is Divdown free to use?
Yes. Divdown is 100% free to use. You can save online videos in HD with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or download limits.
Do I need to log in to any account?
No login is required. Simply paste the public video link into Divdown and save it securely to your device. We never ask for your password or personal details.
Can I save videos in HD quality?
Yes. Divdown supports saving videos in high definition, including 1080p when available. It works on all devices, including mobile, tablet, and desktop.
Why did my video fail to process?
Please ensure the video link is correct and the content is set to public. A stable internet connection is also required. Divdown only processes publicly available videos for personal, offline viewing.
Is Divdown safe and private to use?
Yes. Divdown is secure and respects your privacy. We use SSL encryption, do not require account login, and do not collect, store, or share any personal data or downloaded content. All processing happens in your browser.
